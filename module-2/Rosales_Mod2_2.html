Introduction

Who is Edgar Rosales? Where did he come from? Let us try to shed some light upon him. We will look at three different period in His life; childhood, service, and carer.

The life, of Edgar Daniel Rosales Fierros began in the early months of 1973, in the city of Tijuana in Mexico. His childhood spent between living in two cities, Tijuana and Guadalajara. During his formative teen years he arrived in the United States in the city of Los Angeles. Once again he split his time between two cities, Los Angeles and Long Beach. He attended Long Beach Polytechnic High School, and Washington Preparatory High School. He graduated from Washington Preparatory HS and then joined the Army.

In 1992, after having spent some time in community college, Edgar enlisted in the Army. Thus began his eight years of service. During his first service term he was deployed to Somalia in support of operation Restore Hope. After he returned to Fort Devens, and received orders to report to Fort Riley, while serving there he re-enlisted for another four years of service.He then was stationed in Germany for those four years.

After Edgar received his honorary discharge, he returned to Los Angeles, where after floundering for a short time decided to start a carer in Information technology and began working for the LAUSD E-Rate project, under a sub-contractor of SBC. During that time he learned to program cisco switches and routers. Since then he has worked on large and international networks, touching the lives of people all over the world.
